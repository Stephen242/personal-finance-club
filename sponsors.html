Our Sponsors

Proud partners empowering financial education

We are grateful for the support of our valued sponsors who share our vision of empowering UNSW students with financial literacy and investment knowledge. Through their generous support, we are able to provide high-quality educational events, resources, and opportunities to our members.

Arc UNSW

Arc is UNSW's student organization, providing support, funding, and resources to enhance student life. Their backing enables us to deliver impactful events and initiatives that benefit the UNSW community.

Striver

A platform designed to help individuals navigate their career paths through coaching, skill-building resources, and job placement services. They provide tailored career advice and connect them with opportunities that align with one's goals.

Sharesies

A popular investment platform that allows users to easily buy and sell shares in companies, and ETFs. It offers a user-friendly interface and educational resources, making investing accessible to people of all experience levels and communities.

WeMoney

A smart money management app that connects all your financial accounts in one place and tracks your overall financial health. They assist people to pay down debts faster, track bills, bank accounts and remove unnecessary subscriptions.

UniSuper

UniSuper is Australia's dedicated superannuation fund for the higher education and research sector. They provide expert financial advice and retirement planning services, helping members secure their financial future through smart investment strategies.

Fiducian

Fiducian is a wealth management company providing comprehensive financial planning, investment management, and superannuation services. They focus on delivering personalized financial solutions to help clients achieve their long-term financial goals.
