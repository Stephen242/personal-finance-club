About Us

Established in 2023, the UNSW Personal Finance Club (PFC) is a passionate student society at the University of New South Wales. With a thriving and growing community, PFC is dedicated to equipping UNSW students with practical knowledge in budgeting, investing, tax, and financial wellbeing. Our mission is to empower students to take control of their finances and build a confident financial future.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students to take action towards a financially secure future. We hope to help facilitate students with their transition from university to adult life, with specific attention to the area of personal finance as it enables them to better pursue their various goals.
